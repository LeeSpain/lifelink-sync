# Privacy Policy for ICE SOS

**Last Updated:** February 27, 2026

## 1. Introduction

ICE SOS ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our emergency response platform.

**IMPORTANT:** ICE SOS is an emergency assistance platform. By using our service, you understand that:
- Emergency information may be shared with emergency services and your designated contacts
- Location data is critical for emergency response
- Time is critical in emergencies - we prioritize rapid response over extensive consent processes

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

- **Account Information:** Name, email address, phone number, password
- **Profile Information:** Date of birth, medical information (optional), allergies, medications
- **Emergency Contacts:** Names, phone numbers, email addresses, relationships
- **Location Data:** GPS coordinates, address information during emergencies
- **Communication Data:** Messages, voice call recordings, chat transcripts

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, browser type
- **Usage Data:** Pages visited, features used, time spent on platform
- **Location Data:** Real-time location during emergency activations
- **Call Data:** Call duration, participants, timestamps
- **Technical Data:** IP address, cookies, log files

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

- **Emergency Services:** Confirmation of emergency response
- **Payment Processors:** Transaction data (if applicable)
- **Analytics Providers:** Usage statistics and performance metrics

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Emergency Response

- Notify your emergency contacts immediately
- Share your location with responders
- Coordinate emergency assistance
- Record emergency calls for quality assurance
- Provide context to emergency coordinators (AI and human)

### 3.2 Service Operations

- Create and manage your account
- Process payments and subscriptions
- Send service-related notifications
- Provide customer support
- Improve our platform and develop new features

### 3.3 Communication

- Send emergency alerts and notifications
- Respond to your inquiries
- Send service updates and important notices
- Marketing communications (with your consent)

### 3.4 Legal and Safety

- Comply with legal obligations
- Protect against fraud and abuse
- Enforce our Terms of Service
- Respond to legal requests

## 4. How We Share Your Information

### 4.1 Emergency Situations

**When you activate emergency services, we immediately share:**
- Your name and contact information
- Your current location (GPS coordinates and address)
- Your medical information (if provided)
- Your emergency contacts

**Recipients may include:**
- Your designated emergency contacts
- Emergency services (911, 112, etc.) if you request
- Our AI coordinator (Clara) for emergency coordination
- Emergency dispatch centers

### 4.2 Service Providers

We share information with trusted third-party providers:
- **Twilio:** For voice calls and SMS messaging
- **OpenAI:** For AI coordination (anonymized when possible)
- **Supabase:** For secure data storage
- **Payment processors:** For subscription management
- **Analytics providers:** For platform improvement

### 4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or to:
- Respond to legal process (subpoenas, court orders)
- Protect our rights and property
- Prevent illegal activity or harm
- Comply with regulatory requirements

### 4.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale, your information may be transferred to the new entity.

## 5. Data Retention

- **Account Data:** Retained while your account is active + 7 years after deletion
- **Emergency Records:** Retained for 7 years for legal and safety purposes
- **Call Recordings:** Retained for 90 days for quality assurance
- **Location Data:** Retained for 30 days after emergency incidents
- **Analytics Data:** Aggregated data retained indefinitely

**You can request data deletion** by contacting privacy@icesos.com. Note that some data must be retained for legal compliance.

## 6. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

### 6.1 Access and Portability

- Request a copy of your personal data
- Download your data in a portable format

### 6.2 Correction and Deletion

- Correct inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)

### 6.3 Consent and Objection

- Withdraw consent for marketing communications
- Object to processing based on legitimate interests
- Opt-out of data sales (we do not sell your data)

### 6.4 Restriction

- Request restricted processing of your data
- Limit how we use your information

**To exercise your rights:** Contact privacy@icesos.com or use the settings in your account.

## 7. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- **Encryption:** All data encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest (AES-256)
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access to sensitive information
- **Authentication:** Multi-factor authentication available
- **Monitoring:** 24/7 security monitoring and alerts
- **Audits:** Regular security audits and penetration testing

**However, no system is 100% secure.** We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 8. Location Data and Emergency Services

### 8.1 Location Collection

- **During Emergencies:** Continuous location tracking when SOS is activated
- **Background Location:** Only collected when explicitly enabled and consented
- **Accuracy:** We use GPS, Wi-Fi, and cell tower data for accuracy

### 8.2 Location Sharing

Your location is shared with:
- Emergency contacts immediately upon SOS activation
- Emergency services if you request connection
- Our AI coordinator for situational awareness

### 8.3 Location Retention

- Emergency location data retained for 30 days
- Historical location data can be deleted upon request
- No location tracking when app is not in use (unless background mode enabled)

## 9. Children's Privacy

ICE SOS is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child, contact us immediately at privacy@icesos.com.

**Teen Users (13-17):** Parental consent required. Parents have access to their teen's emergency information.
