## Detailed Notes

### JiAi / Immecare
- Full OEM manufacturer with existing pendant/wearable product line
- Can build to spec: custom casing, electronics, firmware
- Likely has BLE module experience and existing CE certifications
- Higher MOQ but full control over design and integration
- Best long-term partner for scaling

### Trelawear
- Existing safety jewellery brand — product already designed
- White label option means faster time to first shipment
- May offer exclusive territory rights (Spain, Netherlands)
- Unknown if they support third-party app integration
- Best for getting product to customers fastest

### ResQ Jewelry
- Bluetooth safety pendant already in their product line
- Co-branding or white label possible
- Integration depth unknown — may need custom firmware work
- Good option if their existing product meets our BLE spec

### JewelryOEM (casing only)
- Silver casing manufacturer — no electronics
- Would need to source BLE module separately and assemble
- More complex supply chain but full design control
- Lower MOQ on the casing side

### Zearrow (casing only)
- Ethical silver sourcing — good for marketing story
- Very low MOQ (100+) — good for prototyping
- No electronics — same assembly challenge as JewelryOEM
- Good for premium limited editions

## Recommended Strategy

### Phase 1: Dual Approach (Immediate)
1. **Contact JiAi/Immecare** — request full product catalogue, pricing at 500/1k/5k volumes, and sample timeline
2. **Contact Trelawear** — explore white label deal for fastest route to market

### Phase 2: Evaluate (Week 2-3)
- Compare responses on pricing, MOQ, lead times, and integration capability
- Request samples from both
- Evaluate BLE SDK/API documentation quality

### Phase 3: Decision (Week 4)
- **If speed matters most:** Go with Trelawear white label for first 500 units
- **If customisation matters most:** Go with JiAi/Immecare for full OEM
- **Hybrid option:** Launch with Trelawear white label while JiAi builds custom v2
