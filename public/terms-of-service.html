1. About LifeLink Sync

LifeLink Sync is an emergency protection platform designed for individuals and families. Our platform provides the following features:

  • SOS alerts with real-time location sharing
  • Live location sharing within your family circle
  • Family circle coordination and management
  • CLARA AI assistant for emergency guidance, wellbeing check-ins, and medication reminders
  • Wellbeing monitoring and daily check-ins
  • Medication reminder and adherence tracking

LifeLink Sync is NOT an emergency service. It is NOT a medical provider. It is NOT a replacement for 112, 999, 911, or any other local emergency number.

IMPORTANT: LifeLink Sync supplements but does NOT replace emergency services. In any life-threatening situation, ALWAYS call your local emergency number first: 112 (Europe), 999 (UK), 911 (US). Do not rely solely on this application for emergency response.
