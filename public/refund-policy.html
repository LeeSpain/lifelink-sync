Refund Policy - LifeLink Sync

Effective Date: 17 March 2026

Last Updated: 17 March 2026

Next Review: 17 September 2026

Summary (Plain English)

  • New subscribers: full refund within 7 days of first charge.
  • After 7 days: no refunds except where required by EU law.
  • Annual plans: no pro-rata refunds mid-period.
  • Gift subscriptions: non-refundable once activated by recipient.
  • EU consumers have a 14-day withdrawal right.

NOTE: This is a template document. A qualified lawyer must review before it is relied upon legally.

1. New Subscriber Refund

  • If you are a new subscriber making your first payment, you are entitled to a full refund within 7 days of your first charge.
  • This applies to both monthly and annual plans.
  • The 7-day refund window starts from the date your card is first charged, not from account creation or trial start.
  • To request a refund, email billing@lifelink-sync.com with your account email address.

2. Monthly Plans

  • After the 7-day new subscriber window, no partial refunds are issued for unused days within a monthly billing period.
  • You can cancel at any time. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current monthly billing period.
  • You retain full access until the end of the paid period.

3. Annual Plans

  • Annual plans are billed as a single payment of €99.90.
  • After the 7-day new subscriber window, pro-rata refunds are not available on annual plans unless required by applicable law.
  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current annual billing period.
  • You retain full access until the end of the paid period.

4. Trial-to-Paid Conversions

  • If you convert from a free trial to a paid subscription, the 7-day refund window starts from the date of your first actual charge.
  • If you cancel during the free trial, no charge is made and no refund is needed.

5. Gift Subscriptions

  • Before activation: If the recipient has not yet activated the gift, the buyer may request a full refund by contacting billing@lifelink-sync.com.
  • After activation: Gift subscriptions are non-refundable once the recipient has created an account and activated the gift.
  • Gift vouchers that have not been redeemed may be refunded to the original buyer.

6. Add-Ons

  • Add-on subscriptions (Family Links, Daily Wellbeing, Medication Reminder) can be cancelled at any time from your dashboard.
  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle.
  • No partial refunds for unused portions of an add-on billing period.

7. EU Consumer Rights

  • Under the EU Consumer Rights Directive, EU consumers have a 14-day right of withdrawal from the date of purchase.
  • If you have used the service during the withdrawal period with your prior express consent, you may be required to pay for the service used up to the point of withdrawal.
  • To exercise your right of withdrawal, contact legal@lifelink-sync.com.

8. How to Request a Refund

  • Email: billing@lifelink-sync.com
  • Include: your account email address and reason for refund.
  • Processing time: refunds are processed within 5–10 business days and returned to the original payment method.
  • Stripe transaction fees are non-refundable.

9. Exceptions

  • If LifeLink Sync experiences a service outage affecting your account for more than 72 consecutive hours, you may request a pro-rata credit for the affected period.
  • Refunds required by applicable law (including EU consumer protection law) will always be honoured regardless of the terms above.
