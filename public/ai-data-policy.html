Summary

  • CLARA is an AI assistant — not a medical professional or emergency responder
  • CLARA processes your conversations, profile data, and health data (when add-ons are active)
  • Your conversation history is stored for 90 days to provide contextual responses, then deleted
  • Your data is NOT used to train external AI models
  • You can opt out of CLARA processing at any time by not using the chat or deactivating add-ons
WARNING: CLARA is an artificial intelligence system. CLARA does NOT provide medical advice, make diagnoses, or replace healthcare professionals. CLARA's wellbeing reports are AI-generated and have NOT been reviewed by clinical professionals. Always consult your doctor or healthcare provider for medical decisions.

1. What is CLARA

CLARA (Connected Lifeline And Response Assistant) is LifeLink Sync's AI-powered assistant.

  • CLARA helps with: emergency protection guidance, daily wellbeing check-ins, medication reminders, and general platform assistance
  • CLARA operates via text chat within the LifeLink Sync platform
  • CLARA can also operate via voice for tablet dashboard users
  • Voice activation on the tablet dashboard processes audio in real-time to detect emergency phrases — audio is NOT stored
  • CLARA is powered by large language model technology
  • CLARA is NOT a human — all interactions are with an AI system

2. What Data CLARA Processes

Data Category When Processed Purpose
Conversation messages Every chat interaction To provide relevant responses
Your profile data When you interact with CLARA To personalise responses (name, preferences)
Health data When Daily Wellbeing add-on is active To conduct wellbeing check-ins (mood, sleep, pain, general wellbeing)
Medication data When Medication Reminder add-on is active To send reminders and log adherence
Emergency contact info When discussing emergency setup To help configure your protection
Location data During emergency situations only To coordinate emergency response
Family circle data When answering questions about your circle To provide accurate family status information
Voice activation events When tablet dashboard voice mode is active To detect emergency phrases and trigger SOS alerts

3. How CLARA Stores and Uses Data

Conversation Memory

  • CLARA stores conversation history to provide contextual, relevant responses
  • Conversation memory is retained for a maximum of 90 days
  • After 90 days, conversation data is automatically deleted
  • Memory types include: short-term (current session context) and long-term (recurring preferences)
  • Memory has an expiration date set at creation — expired memory is purged automatically
  • Voice activation: audio is processed in real-time by on-device or edge processing and is NOT stored, recorded, or transmitted to our servers. Only event metadata (timestamp, trigger type, confidence score) is retained for 30 days

Knowledge Base

  • CLARA has a knowledge base of general information about LifeLink Sync features
  • This knowledge base does NOT contain your personal data
  • Knowledge base entries are curated by our team and available in multiple languages

Learning Queue

  • When CLARA encounters topics it cannot fully address, these may be queued for human review
  • Queued items are anonymised before review
  • This helps us improve CLARA's knowledge base over time
  • No personal health data is included in learning queue items

Vector Embeddings

  • CLARA uses vector embeddings (mathematical representations) for efficient information retrieval
  • Embeddings are generated from knowledge base content, NOT from your personal conversations
  • This technology helps CLARA find relevant information quickly

4. CLARA Complete Reports

  • When both Daily Wellbeing AND Medication Reminder add-ons are active, CLARA generates weekly reports
  • Reports combine: wellbeing check-in data + medication adherence data
  • Reports are sent to: the member AND all linked family circle members
  • Reports are entirely AI-generated
  • Reports are NOT reviewed, validated, or approved by any medical professional
  • Reports should be used for informational purposes only
  • Reports do NOT constitute medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment recommendations
  • Always share reports with your healthcare provider for professional interpretation

5. AI Model and Provider Details

  • CLARA uses large language model (LLM) technology for natural language understanding and generation
  • Model configuration (temperature, response length, system behaviour) is managed by LifeLink Sync administrators
  • The AI model provider processes conversation data to generate responses
  • Model provider data processing agreements are in place

Conversation data sent to the AI model provider is:

  • Transmitted securely via encrypted connection
  • Processed for the sole purpose of generating a response
  • NOT stored by the model provider beyond the processing session
  • NOT used to train, fine-tune, or improve the provider's AI models

6. Your Data is NOT Used for AI Training

  • We do not use your personal data, conversations, health data, or any other information to train AI models
  • Your data is processed solely to provide you with CLARA's services
  • The AI model provider does not retain or train on your data
  • This commitment applies to all data categories: conversations, health, medical, location, and personal

6a. AI Emergency Detection and False Positives

  • CLARA may interpret certain phrases or patterns as emergency indicators
  • False positive detections (non-emergency events flagged as emergencies) are possible and may trigger alerts to your family circle
  • You can cancel a false alert immediately through the app
  • Repeated false positive patterns are logged to improve detection accuracy — these logs contain event metadata only, not conversation content
  • If you experience frequent false positives, contact support@lifelink-sync.com for configuration adjustment

7. GDPR Article 22 — Automated Decision Making

  • CLARA does NOT make decisions that produce legal effects concerning you
  • CLARA does NOT make decisions that similarly significantly affect you
  • CLARA's role is advisory and informational only
  • Wellbeing assessments are suggestions, not binding determinations
  • Medication reminders are notifications, not medical prescriptions
  • Emergency coordination is supportive, not directive — you make all emergency decisions

Your right to human review: You can request that any CLARA output, recommendation, or assessment be reviewed by a human member of our team. Contact ai-privacy@lifelink-sync.com.

8. AI Configuration and Administration

LifeLink Sync administrators can configure CLARA's behaviour:

  • Response style and tone
  • System prompt and personality
  • Maximum response length
  • Model selection
  • Rate limiting (to prevent abuse)
  • Logging settings (enable/disable conversation logging)

CLARA has restricted patterns — certain topics CLARA cannot discuss, with predefined safe responses.

CLARA visibility is controlled by route — CLARA appears only on appropriate pages.

9. Your Rights Regarding AI Data

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of all CLARA conversation data we hold about you
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your CLARA conversation history at any time
  • Opt out: Stop using CLARA at any time — simply don't open the chat. No data will be collected
  • Deactivate add-ons: Cancel Daily Wellbeing and/or Medication Reminder to stop health data processing
  • Human review: Request human review of any CLARA output or decision
  • Object: Object to AI processing of your data under GDPR Article 21
  • Portability: Request your CLARA data in a machine-readable format

To exercise any right, contact ai-privacy@lifelink-sync.com. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Security of AI Data

  • All conversations with CLARA are encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3)
  • Stored conversation data is encrypted at rest (AES-256)
  • Access to AI data is restricted to authorised personnel only
  • AI processing logs are maintained for security audit purposes
  • Regular security reviews of AI data handling practices

11. Children and AI

  • CLARA is not designed for users under 16
  • Users aged 13–15 may interact with CLARA only with verified parental consent
  • CLARA does not knowingly process data from children under 13
  • Parents/guardians can request deletion of their child's AI interaction data

12. Changes to This Policy

  • We will provide 30 days' notice for material changes to how CLARA processes data
  • Notice provided via email and in-app notification
  • Continued use of CLARA after the notice period constitutes acceptance
