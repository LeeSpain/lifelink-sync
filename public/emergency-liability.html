IN ANY LIFE-THREATENING EMERGENCY, ALWAYS CALL EMERGENCY SERVICES FIRST:

  • Europe: 112
  • United Kingdom: 999
  • United States: 911
  • Spain: 112 (Emergencias)

LifeLink Sync is a supplementary coordination tool. It does NOT dispatch emergency responders, provide medical treatment, or replace professional emergency services. NEVER delay calling emergency services because you are using this application.

Summary

  • LifeLink Sync helps coordinate emergency response but does NOT replace calling 112/999/911
  • We are NOT an emergency service, medical provider, or first responder
  • The service depends on technology (internet, GPS, battery) which can fail
  • You are responsible for keeping your emergency contacts and medical data accurate
  • Our maximum liability is limited to fees you have paid in the preceding 12 months

1. What LifeLink Sync Provides

Our emergency features include:

  • SOS alert system — sends notifications to your emergency contacts with your location
  • Location sharing — shares your GPS coordinates with family circle during emergencies
  • Medical information access — makes your stored health data available to emergency contacts
  • Family circle coordination — keeps your family informed during an emergency
  • Emergency contact notifications — alerts your designated contacts via email, push notification
  • CLARA AI assistant — provides emergency guidance and coordination support

2. What LifeLink Sync Does NOT Provide

We explicitly do NOT:

  • Dispatch ambulances, police, fire services, or any emergency responders
  • Replace or substitute for calling 112, 999, 911, or any emergency number
  • Provide medical advice, diagnoses, or treatment recommendations
  • Monitor your location continuously for emergencies (SOS must be manually triggered)
  • Guarantee that emergency contacts will receive or respond to alerts
  • Guarantee the accuracy of GPS location data
  • Provide 24/7 staffed emergency monitoring (CLARA is AI, not human staff)
  • Guarantee service availability (see Technology Dependencies)

3. Technology Dependencies

The service relies on technology that can fail. You acknowledge:

  • Internet connectivity: SOS alerts require an active internet connection (Wi-Fi or mobile data)
  • GPS accuracy: Location accuracy varies by device, environment, and conditions. Indoor accuracy may be poor. GPS may be unavailable in basements, tunnels, or remote areas
  • Device battery: If your device battery dies, the service cannot function
  • Network coverage: Mobile network coverage varies by location and carrier
  • Server availability: While we target high availability, server outages can occur
  • Push notification delivery: Delivery depends on the recipient's device, network, and notification settings
  • Email delivery: Email alerts may be delayed or filtered as spam by recipients' email providers
  • Browser compatibility: PWA features may work differently across browsers and devices

4. Your Responsibilities

You agree to:

  • Always call 112/999/911 first in any life-threatening emergency
  • Keep your emergency contact information accurate and current
  • Keep your medical and health profile data up to date
  • Test your SOS system regularly using the safe test mode
  • Grant location permissions to the application for emergency features to work
  • Ensure your device is charged and has internet connectivity
  • Inform your emergency contacts that they are designated in your profile
  • NOT rely solely on LifeLink Sync for emergency response
  • Report any service malfunctions promptly to support@lifelink-sync.com

11. Emergency Data Sharing

During an active SOS event:

  • Your stored health data (blood type, allergies, conditions, medications) is shared with your emergency contacts
  • Your real-time GPS location is shared with your emergency contacts
  • This sharing occurs automatically — you cannot prevent it during an active SOS
  • This sharing is lawful under GDPR Article 9(2)(c) — vital interests
  • After the SOS is resolved, sharing stops. Emergency records are retained for 7 years
  • You will receive a summary of what was shared after each SOS event
